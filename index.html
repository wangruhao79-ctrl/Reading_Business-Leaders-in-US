Four Technology Leaders Who Changed the World

A. Steve Jobs

Steve Jobs was born in California, USA, in 1955. In 1976, he co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak. Jobs believed that technology should be simple, beautiful, and easy to use. His attention to design and user experience helped Apple create famous products such as the Macintosh computer, the iPod, the iPhone, and the iPad.

When Apple first started, personal computers were not common in many homes. Jobs wanted to create products that ordinary people could use easily. His ideas helped change the way people listen to music, communicate, work, and access information. Many of Apple's products became best-sellers around the world.

Although he faced difficulties during his career and even left Apple for several years, he later returned to the company and led it to great success. Today, Jobs is remembered as one of the most creative and influential business leaders in history.

B. Mark Zuckerberg

Mark Zuckerberg developed an interest in computers when he was young. While studying at Harvard University, he created Facebook, a website that helped people communicate and share information online.

What began as a small project for university students quickly grew into one of the largest social media platforms in the world. Millions of people use Facebook every day to stay in touch with friends and family, share photos, and learn about current events.

Zuckerberg believes technology can connect people across countries and cultures. He has also invested in projects related to artificial intelligence and virtual reality. Today, he remains one of the most influential figures in the technology industry.

C. Elon Musk

Elon Musk was born in South Africa and later moved to North America. He is the CEO of Tesla and SpaceX. Through Tesla, he promotes electric vehicles and renewable energy. Through SpaceX, he works on making space travel more affordable.

Musk believes that technology can help solve some of the world's biggest challenges. His work with Tesla encourages people to use cleaner forms of transportation, while SpaceX develops advanced rockets that can be reused, helping reduce the cost of space missions.

Musk is famous for setting ambitious goals. He hopes that one day humans will be able to live on other planets. Although many of his projects are challenging, he continues to inspire people with his innovative ideas and determination.

D. Bill Gates

Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft in 1975. The company developed software that became widely used around the world, including the Windows operating system. Microsoft's success helped make personal computers common in homes and offices.

As Microsoft grew, Gates became one of the world's most successful entrepreneurs. His work in software transformed the way people use computers for education, communication, and business.

Later in life, Gates focused more on philanthropy. Through the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation, he supports projects that improve global health, education, and poverty reduction. Today, he is respected for both his business achievements and charitable contributions.
